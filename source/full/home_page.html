What is Computer Science About?

Computer Science is the study of computers and computational systems. Unlike electrical and computer engineers, computer scientists deal mostly with software and software systems; this includes their theory, design, development, and application.

Careers in Computer Science

There are many variations.One of the most critical decisions you will make in your education centers on finding your niche in computer science. Researching available options can be overwhelming and exhausting, but honing in on what you want to do and pursuing it relentlessly will give you the best chance of success.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create the most conducive environment for quality academic and research oriented undergraduate and postgraduate education in computer science prepare the students for a globalized technology society and orient them towards serving the society.

Our Philosophy

Philosophically, Computer is concerned with issues that arise from reflection upon the nature and practice of the academic discipline of Computer Science. It is not just about programming; indeed, Computer Science would be better described as being concerned with the meta-activity that is associated with programming. More generally, and more precisely, it is occupied with the design, development and investigation of the concepts and methodologies that facilitate and aid the specification, development, implementation, and analysis of computational systems.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to prepare students to be research and development leaders in computer and computer prgramming. The program also provides leadership in the application of high technology to improve the educational experience. Provide a learning ambience to enhance innovations, problem solving skills, leadership qualities, team-spirit and ethical responsibilities.
