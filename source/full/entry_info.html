ENTRY INFORMATIONS

Entry Requirements

The Department of Computer Science admits three categories of students for her B. Sc. Computer Science Program.

  1. UTME Candidates: In addition to the general entry requirements approved for admission into Nigerian Universities, candidates must have passed SSCE/GCE O'Level or its equivalent at Credit level in English Language, Mathematics, Physics, plus two other Science Subjects, in addition to an acceptable pass in UTME test/screening. A credit in further Mathematics will be an advantage.
  2. Post-Pre-degree Candidates: Students who have successfully completed the University's one year (Pre-degree Science program, are qualified to be admitted into the B. Sc. Degree program in Computer Science. In particular, qualified post-pre-degree candidates must have
    • Passed all the five (5) subjects, Mathematics, English Language, Biology, Chemistry and Physics, they registered for in the Pre-degree year.
    • Obtained a minimum grade of "C" in the O'Level course (s) not passed at credit level in the SSCE/GCE O'Level.
    • Score a minimum of 15 points, or as may be fixed by the Directorate of Pre-degree.
  3. Direct Entry Candidates: Direct Entry Candidates must have
    • Two A‘ Level papers in core science subjects including Mathematics.
    • NCE with least a merit in Mathematics and one other science subject:
    • Diploma in Computer Science or Diploma in Statistics with Computer Application from University of Calabar or any other recognized University.
    • OND/HND with an Upper Credit in any of the following Computer Science, Engineering, Mathematics. All Direct Entry candidates must satisfy the requirements in (I) above.
